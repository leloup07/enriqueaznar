Selected experience

Cases that shaped
the practice.

Three decades of work at the frontier of governance crises, regulatory innovation and organisational transformation.

I
Telecommunications · EMEA

Corruption crisis at a NASDAQ multinational

Management Board member during a $13.5bn telecoms group's response to a serious bribery and corruption crisis across multiple jurisdictions. Oversaw the design and implementation of the post-crisis compliance architecture under DOJ monitorship, managing the tension between US enforcement expectations and European operational realities.

Outcome
Full monitorship compliance. Global programme restructured across 10+ jurisdictions.
II
Fintech · Spain · CNMV

First debt tokenisation in the CNMV Regulatory Sandbox

Co-founder and lead of the legal and regulatory design of the first Spanish real world asset tokenisation company approved in the CNMV Fintech Sandbox. Full development of the legal structure, investor documentation and regulatory interaction framework under the DLT Pilot Regime and Spanish securities law.

Resultado
First cohort approval. Regulatory precedent under CNMV supervision.
III
Technology · Global operations

Culture transformation in a crisis-hit technology group

Led a values-based culture transformation programme at a major telecoms technology company operating in volatile, complex markets. Replaced conventional management hierarchies with distributed governance models, redesigning how the organisation made decisions, allocated accountability and measured integrity.

Resultado
Adopted across three operating regions. Embedded in board reporting and performance metrics.
